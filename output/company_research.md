# Company Research and Interview Preparation for Agence Française de Développement (AFD) - Data Scientist Position

## Company Overview

**Name:** Agence Française de Développement (AFD)  
**Location:** Paris, France  
**Industry:** Public sector, Finance, International Development  
**Size:** Medium to Large (approximately 3,000 employees as of 2020)  
**Mission:** AFD's mission is to promote sustainable economic growth, improve living conditions, and contribute to environmental protection in alignment with France's development policies. The agency supports over 2,500 development projects in 108 countries, focusing on climate change, poverty reduction, and stabilization of post-crisis regions.  
**Values:** Solidarity, sustainability, global partnerships, tailored solutions for diverse countries.

### Recent News
- AFD is actively engaged in climate finance initiatives, contributing approximately US$3 billion annually.
- Recent projects include partnerships with the Green Climate Fund, focusing on climate resilience and sustainable urban development in multiple countries.

## Role Context

**Job Title:** Data Scientist  
**Location:** Paris, France  
**Employment Type:** Permanent, Senior Level  
**Job Summary:** The Data Scientist will focus on analyzing socio-economic data and impact metrics within AFD, developing data models to facilitate decision-making in various sectors related to AFD's mission.

### Key Qualifications and Technologies:
- Strong expertise in data analysis and economic theories.
- Proficiency in Python and SQL for data management and analysis.
- A Master's degree and over 5 years of relevant experience are expected.

### Team Structure:
- Likely part of the research and data analytics team, liaising with experts across diverse projects.
- Collaborative environment involving stakeholders from various international development sectors.

## Anticipated Interview Topics
1. **Technical Proficiency:** 
   - Questions related to data analysis using Python and SQL.
   - Case studies or scenarios involving socio-economic data interpretation.

2. **Domain Knowledge:**
   - Understanding of economic theories and their application in development contexts.

3. **Problem-Solving:**
   - Approaches to developing data models that address AFD's objectives in impact evaluation.

4. **Mission Alignment:**
   - Discussion on climate change mitigation strategies and their alignment with personal data science projects.

## Suggested Candidate Questions
1. **Project Impact:**
   - "Can you provide examples of previous data projects that have significantly influenced AFD's development strategies?"

2. **Team and Culture:**
   - "What is the typical composition of a team within AFD, and how do cross-functional teams collaborate?"

3. **Growth and Development:**
   - "What opportunities does AFD offer for continued professional development and career advancement?"

4. **Success Metrics:**
   - "How does AFD measure the success of its data-driven projects, and what role do data scientists play in this evaluation?"

5. **Innovations in Data Usage:**
   - "How is AFD currently innovating in the use of data science to address development and climate challenges?"

This comprehensive overview provides insights into AFD and prepares the candidate for meaningful discussions during the interview process.
